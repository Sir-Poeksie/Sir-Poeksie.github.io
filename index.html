Meet Sir Poeksie — The Vision in Code, the Brand in Motion
Mpumelelo Theophilas Nxazonke, affectionately known as “Sir Poeksie,” hails from the vibrant heart of Benoni, a humble town east of Johannesburg, South Africa. But make no mistake—his dreams are anything but small.

Fueled by vision, precision, and unapologetic ambition, Sir Poeksie is a full-stack software developer and AI & Cloud Technology enthusiast with a flair for turning bold ideas into functional, futuristic solutions. He codes with purpose and designs with soul — seamlessly merging technology, innovation, and brand identity into intelligent, intentional experiences.
Whether it’s crafting elegant eCommerce platforms for fashion-forward brands, developing AI-powered health applications, or architecting centralized data systems for impact-driven organizations, every project is stitched with his signature blend of creativity, strategy, and disruptive innovation.
Currently pursuing a BSc in Information Technology with a major in Emerging Technologies, and enrolled in the prestigious Power Learn Project Software Development Programme, he's also on track to completing his AWS certifications — fortifying a tech stack that's as deep as it is dynamic.
But more than just code, Sir Poeksie is a brand architect, a problem solver, and a visionary who sees beauty in structure and meaning in design. He's not just building apps — he’s building futures.
